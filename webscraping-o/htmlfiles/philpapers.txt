       <h2 class="home" style="margin-top:40px;">
        Welcome to PhilPapers
       </h2>
       <div id="intro">
        <div class="introduction">
         PhilPapers is a comprehensive index and bibliography of philosophy maintained by the community of philosophers. 
    We monitor all sources of research content in philosophy, including
         <a href="/journals/">
          journals
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="/recent?preset=books">
          books
         </a>
         ,
         <a href="/archives/list.html">
          open access archives
         </a>
         , and
         <a href="/pages/list.html">
          personal pages
         </a>
         maintained by academics.  
    We also host the largest
         <a href="https://philarchive.org">
          open access archive in philosophy
         </a>
         .  
    Our index currently contains 2,566,210 entries categorized in 5,671 categories. PhilPapers has over 270,000 registered users.
