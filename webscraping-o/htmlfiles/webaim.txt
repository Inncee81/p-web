    <h1>
     We have web accessibility in mind
    </h1>
    <p id="mission">
     Expanding the potential of the web for people with disabilities by empowering individuals and organizations to create accessible content.
    </p>
    <div id="prodserv">
     <div id="training">
      <h2>
       <a href="/services/training/">
        Accessibility Training
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Whether here in Utah or on-site at your organization, WebAIM can provide web and document training to fit your needs.
      </p>
     </div>
     <div id="certification">
      <h2>
       <a href="/services/certification/">
        Accessible Site Certification
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       As a respected third party accessibility expert, WebAIM can evaluate and certify your site to established web accessibility guidelines.
      </p>
     </div>
     <div id="consulting">
      <h2>
       <a href="/services/consulting/">
        Technical Assistance
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       Need assistance implementing accessibility? WebAIM's expert staff can provide the assistance you need.
      </p>
     </div>
     <div id="monitoring">
      <h2>
       <a href="/services/monitoring/">
        Evaluation and Reporting
       </a>
      </h2>
      <p>
       We can provide reports to help you know how accessible your site is and how to make it better.
      </p>

     <h2>
      Current Features
     </h2>
     <h3>
      Survey of Web Accessibility Practitioners
     </h3>
     <p>
      A new WebAIM survey is open for anyone that implements accessibility. This is a follow-up to previous surveys. Please take a few minutes to complete the survey to provide valuable data and insight into the web accessibility field and practices.
     </p>
     <p class="more">
      <a href="/projects/practitionersurvey3/">
       Take the Practitioners Survey
      </a>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Scholarship Program for WebAIM's Accessible Documents Course
     </h3>
     <p>
      All individuals with a disability may enroll in the
      <a href="/training/docs/">
       Accessible Documents: Word, PowerPoint, &amp; Acrobat
      </a>
      course without cost through WebAIM's scholarship program.
     </p>
     <p class="more">
      <a href="/training/docs/scholarship">
       Apply for the scholarship
      </a>
     </p>
     <h3>
      Significant WAVE Updates
     </h3>
     <p>
      A new version of the
      <a href="https://wave.webaim.org/">
       WAVE
      </a>
      suite of web accessibility testing tools has been released. This update now gives you more power, more flexibility, and a fresh new interface.
     </p>

      <a href="https://webaim.org/blog/significant-wave-updates/">
