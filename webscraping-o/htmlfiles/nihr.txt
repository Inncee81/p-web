     <h2 class="large-text white-text">
      About Us
     </h2>
     <p class="white-text">
      We are the nation's largest funder of health and care research and provide the people, facilities and technology that enables research to thrive. Working in partnership with the
      <a href="https://www.nhs.uk/">
       NHS
      </a>
      , universities, local government, other research funders, patients and the public, we deliver and enable world-class research that transforms people's lives, promotes economic growth and advances science.
     </p>
     <p class="white-text">
      We are primarily funded by the
      <a href="https://www.gov.uk/government/organisations/department-of-health-and-social-care">
       Department of Health and Social Care
      </a>
      , but also receive UK Aid funding to support research for people in low- and middle-income countries.
     </p>
    </div>
    <div class="about-us-landing-block-item padding-bottom-2rem">
     <a href="/about-us/our-mission/our-mission-and-strategic-workstreams.htm">
      <img alt="" class="padding-bottom-2rem padding-top-2rem img-fluid" src="/layout/img/motifs/corporate/radiating.png"/>
      Our Mission
     </a>
     <p>
      NIHR's mission is to improve the health and wealth of the nation through research. We deliver against this mission through five strategic workstreams.
     </p>
    </div>
