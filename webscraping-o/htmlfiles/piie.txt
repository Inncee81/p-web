        </div>
        <div class="field field--free-text">
         <p>

         <p>
          America’s vaccine production drive in less than a year was a triumph. Inoculating the world will take a COVID-19 trade deal that borrows from the US government success in organizing and subsidizing a complex, sophisticated and geographically dispersed supply chain for manufacturing and distributing the doses.
