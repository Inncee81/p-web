      <p>
       <strong>
        What's open?
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       In England and Northern Ireland, gardens, parks and countryside remain open for local exercise. On 13 March we began to reopen coast and countryside car parks in Wales for local use. The safety of our staff, volunteers and visitors from the nearby community remains our priority and we urge you to book your visits in advance and stay local. Please check the property webpage before you travel.
       <br/>
       <br/>
       <strong>
        What's closed and what's reopening?
       </strong>
      </p>
      <p>
       Following the latest government announcement around the easing of restrictions in England, we plan to open indoor places from 17 May, if government guidance allows it. Cafés are currently takeaway only and we're planning to offer outdoor seating from 12 April and inside seating from 17 May. Shops are currently closed with some due to reopen from 12 April.
       <br/>
       <br/>
