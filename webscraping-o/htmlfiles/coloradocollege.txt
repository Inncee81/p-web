        <h4>
         Untold Stories: Taizo Nakashima
        </h4>

        </p>
        <p>
         Colorado College’s connection with Asia was built, initially, by its international students.
         <a href="other/untoldstories/stories/taizo_nakashima.html">
          <strong>
           Taizo Nakashima
          </strong>
          of Japan, one of only five graduates in 1893
         </a>
         , would go on to acquire degrees in psychology at Harvard University and Cornell University and become an eminent figure in that field in Japan. Nakashima’s story is the latest Untold Stories feature, which sheds light on the stories of marginalized persons who have studied, taught, and worked at Colorado College.
