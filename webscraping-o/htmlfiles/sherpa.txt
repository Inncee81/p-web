      <h1 class="h1_like_h2">
       Research Funders' Open Access Policies
      </h1>
      <p class="strapline">
       Helping authors and institutions make informed and confident decisions in open access publication and compliance.
      </p>
      <h2>
       Sherpa Romeo
      </h2>
      <p>
       Sherpa Romeo enables researchers and librarians to see publishers' conditions for open access archiving on a journal-by-journal basis.
       <br/>
       <a href="https://v2.sherpa.ac.uk/romeo">
        Visit Sherpa Romeo
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Sherpa Juliet
      </h2>
      <p>
       Sherpa Juliet enables researchers and librarians to see funders' conditions for open access publication.
       <br/>
       <a href="https://v2.sherpa.ac.uk/juliet/">
        Visit Sherpa Juliet
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       OpenDOAR
      </h2>
      <p>
       OpenDOAR enables the identification, browsing and search for repositories.
       <br/>
       <a href="https://v2.sherpa.ac.uk/opendoar/">
        Visit OpenDOAR
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Sherpa Fact
      </h2>
      <p>
       Sherpa Fact checks if compliance with funder open access policies can be achieved with a particular journal.
       <br/>
       <a href="https://v2.sherpa.ac.uk/fact">
        Visit Sherpa Fact
       </a>
      </p>
      <h2>
       Sherpa REF
      </h2>
      <p>
       Sherpa REF helps authors and institutions decide whether a journal allows them to comply with the OA REF policy.
       <br/>
       <a href="https://ref.sherpa.ac.uk/">
        Visit Sherpa REF
       </a>
      </p>
     </div>
