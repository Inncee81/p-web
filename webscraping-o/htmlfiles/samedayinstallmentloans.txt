      <h2 class="js-animate anim-left">
       Understanding Installment Loans and How It Works
      </h2>
      <p>
       People nowadays are resorting to installment loans to have enough money for buying a dream car, a home, or for emergency needs like hospitalization. A fixed interest rate is added to the principal amount and must be paid monthly. Applying for this sort of debt is straightforward and fast. Your credit history is a vital factor in getting some funds. But, some lending companies may grant you some money, even when one has a weak credit point.
      </p>
      <h2 id="the-fundamentals-of-online-installment-loans">
       The Fundamentals of Online Installment Loans
      </h2>
      <p>
       You might be earning a steady income, but it is not enough to finance everything you need. Because of this, you are considering to get online installment loans. If you are new in the lending industry, you should be aware that installment debts are either collateralized or non-collateralized.
      </p>
      <p>
       A mortgage loan is an example of collateralized debts, and the house being bought is used as collateral. For some reason you failed to repay the amount borrowed, the firm will take possession of your house as repayment.
      </p>
      <p>
       For non-collateralized debts, the creditor will not ask for collateral. It is quite enticing, but don't be fooled by this. The creditor can impose a more significant interest.
      </p>
      <p>
       Also, the creditworthiness of the borrower is a vital factor in getting approved. Paying for the non-collateralized credit is similar to collateralized debts; you follow a fixed-rate monthly. The loanable cash depends on the salary or monthly income and credit history.
      </p>
      <h2 id="types-of-installment-loans">
       Types of Installment Loans
      </h2>
      <p>
       Online Installment loans are a simplified scheme of getting money, and it is categorized as follows:
      </p>
      <h3 id="personal-loan">
       Personal Loan
      </h3>
      <p>
       Personal credit is applied to meet your different financial needs, such as unexpected funding expenses, and the payment term is within 12 to 96 months. Since it is non-collateralized, a more significant interest rate is imposed, which is much higher as compared to other sorts of debts.
      </p>
      <h3 id="car-loan">
       Car Loan
      </h3>
      <p>
       This is a collateralized borrowing, and you use the vehicle being brought as the guarantee. Payment is made monthly within a specified period. A few creditors will offer a loan with affordable fees. Some offer small installment loans, while others will not.
      </p>
      <p>
       If one needs small installment loans that offer minimal monthly repayment, one can request for debt with extended terms. But it will impose higher interest. It only means that the borrower should pay more within 84-months as compared to buying a vehicle in cash. If a creditor wants to reduce payment and can pay higher monthly dues, choose a loan with a shorter term.
      </p>
      <p>
       If you prefer to buy a house, get a mortgage installment loan online. This sort of loan is repaid monthly within 15 to 30 years. Some mortgage payment varies from month to month, depending on some factors. Others come with fixed interest rates, which means that your monthly payment won't change.
      </p>
      <h2 id="what-is-a-payday-installment-loans">
       What is a Payday Installment Loans?
      </h2>
