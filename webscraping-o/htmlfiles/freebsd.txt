         <h1>
          The FreeBSD Project
         </h1>
         <p>
          FreeBSD is an operating system used to power modern servers,
desktops, and embedded
          <a href="https://www.freebsd.org/platforms/">
           platforms
          </a>
          A large
          <a href="https://docs.FreeBSD.org/en/articles/contributors/#staff-committers/">
           community
          </a>
          has continually developed it for more than thirty
years. Its advanced networking, security, and storage features have
made FreeBSD the platform of choice for many of the
          <a href="https://docs.FreeBSD.org/en/books/handbook/introduction/#introduction-nutshell-users">
           busiest web sites
          </a>
          and most pervasive embedded networking and
storage devices.
         </p>
