          <h1 class="has-text-align-center">
           Get things done with ease, comfort, and control.
          </h1>
          <p class="has-text-align-center">
           An easy and elegant way to use your computer, GNOME 3 is designed to help you have the best possible computing experience.
          </p>
          <div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px">
          </div>
          <hr class="wp-block-separator has-text-color has-background is-style-wide" style="background-color:#2ec27e;color:#2ec27e"/>
          <div aria-hidden="true" class="wp-block-spacer" style="height:20px">
          </div>
          <div class="wp-block-media-text alignwide is-stacked-on-mobile" style="grid-template-columns:41% auto">
           <figure class="wp-block-media-text__media">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-14734 size-full" height="236" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 420px) 100vw, 420px" src="https://wgo2.openshift.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/activities-overview-applications-420x236-1.png" srcset="https://www.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/activities-overview-applications-420x236-1.png 420w, https://www.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/activities-overview-applications-420x236-1-300x169.png 300w" width="420"/>
           </figure>
           <div class="wp-block-media-text__content">
            <h2>
             Simple and Easy to Use
            </h2>
            <p>
             Every part of GNOME 3 has been designed to make it simple and easy to use. The Activities Overview is a simple way to access all your basic tasks. A press of a button is all it takes to view your open windows, launch applications, or check if you have new messages. Having everything in one convenient place means you don’t have to learn your way around a maze of different technologies.
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
          <hr class="wp-block-separator has-text-color has-background is-style-dots" style="background-color:#2ec27e;color:#2ec27e"/>
          <div class="wp-block-media-text alignwide has-media-on-the-right is-stacked-on-mobile" style="grid-template-columns:auto 41%">
           <figure class="wp-block-media-text__media">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-14727 size-full" height="236" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 420px) 100vw, 420px" src="https://wgo2.openshift.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/online-accounts-420x2361-1.png" srcset="https://www.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/online-accounts-420x2361-1.png 420w, https://www.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/online-accounts-420x2361-1-300x169.png 300w" width="420"/>
           </figure>
           <div class="wp-block-media-text__content">
            <h2>
             Intuitive and Efficient
            </h2>
            <p>
             GNOME 3 provides a focused working environment that helps you get things done. It is packed with features that will make you more productive: a powerful search feature that helps you access all your work from one place; side-by-side windows that make it easy to view several documents at the same time; seamless integration with online accounts which allows you to access all your data in one place; and a messaging system that comfortably deals with notifications, letting you quickly respond in place or return to them in a convenient time.
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>
          <hr class="wp-block-separator has-text-color has-background is-style-dots" style="background-color:#2ec27e;color:#2ec27e"/>
          <div class="wp-block-media-text alignwide is-stacked-on-mobile" style="grid-template-columns:41% auto">
           <figure class="wp-block-media-text__media">
            <img alt="" class="wp-image-14728 size-full" height="236" loading="lazy" sizes="(max-width: 420px) 100vw, 420px" src="https://wgo2.openshift.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/weather-420x2361-1.png" srcset="https://www.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/weather-420x2361-1.png 420w, https://www.gnome.org/wp-content/uploads/2020/08/weather-420x2361-1-300x169.png 300w" width="420"/>
           </figure>
           <div class="wp-block-media-text__content">
            <h2>
             Finely Crafted
            </h2>
            <p>
             Every aspect of GNOME 3 has been crafted to fit together as a harmonious whole, providing a consistent and integrated experience. Our attention to detail means a smooth and polished product that lets you do the things you want without getting in the way. GNOME 3 is satisfying to use and beautiful to behold.
            </p>
           </div>
          </div>

          </div>
