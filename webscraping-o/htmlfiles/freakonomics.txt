       <h2>
        <a href="/podcast/fresh-starts/">
         Are You Ready for a Fresh Start? (Ep. 455)
        </a>
       </h2>
       <p>
        <span style="font-weight: 400;">
         Behavioral scientists have been exploring if — and when — a psychological reset can lead to lasting change.
        </span>
        <span style="font-weight: 400;">
         We survey evidence from the London Underground, Major League Baseball, and New Year’s resolutions; we look at accidental fresh starts, forced fresh starts, and fresh starts that backfire. And we wonder: will the pandemic’s end provide the biggest fresh start ever?
        </span>
       </p>
