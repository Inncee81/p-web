      <h2>
       For Syrian refugees, every day is an emergency
      </h2>
      <div class="facts--banner">
       <div class="facts--info">
        <div class="wrapper">
         <h3>
          After 10 years of crisis, life is harder than ever for displaced Syrians. More than 70% of them live in poverty.
         </h3>
         <p>
          COVID-19 is having a disastrous impact on their lives.
          <br/>
          UNHCR has been working on the ground, providing lifesaving support both in Syria and the neighbouring countries, since the start of the crisis.
          <br/>
          Please support our efforts.
         </p>
         <p>
          <strong>
           <a class="btn yellow" href="https://donate.unhcr.org/int/syria/" id="homefeature-donate" target="_blank">
            Help now
           </a>
          </strong>
         </p>
        </div>
       </div>
       <div class="facts--figures">
        <div class="wrapper">
         <h2>
          $200
         </h2>
         <p>
          can provide a family of five in Syria with core relief items such as; mattresses, fleece blankets, a kitchen set and a solar lamp
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          $175
         </h2>
         <p>
          can provide cash assistance to an affected family in Lebanon
         </p>
         <hr/>
         <h2>
          $60
         </h2>
         <p>
          can provide 10 refugees with hygiene kits in Jordan
         </p>
