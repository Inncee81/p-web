      <p>
       A wiki for information about the Casio PT-7 Electronic Musical Instrument.
      </p>
      <p>
       The PT-7 synthesizer has a unique design with a 29 micro-key semi-detachable keyboard using a foil touch sensor, and has 8-note polyphony.
      </p>
