      <h1>
       How To Forward a Port
      </h1>
      <p>
       A port forward is a way of making a computer on your home or business network accessible to computers on the internet, even though they are behind a router. It is commonly used in gaming, security camera setup, voice over ip, and downloading files. After you have forwarded a port you are said to have an
       <strong>
        open port
       </strong>
       .
      </p>
      <h4>
       The easiest way to forward a port is to use our
       <a href="https://portforward.com/store/pfconfig.cgi">
        Network Utilities
       </a>
       suite of tools.
      </h4>
      <br/>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        Port forwards are setup in your router. A summary of the steps to setup a port forward in your router are:
       </p>
       <ol>
        <li>
         Login to your router.
        </li>
        <li>
         Navigate to your routers
         <strong>
          port forwarding
         </strong>
         section, also frequently called
         <strong>
          virtual server
         </strong>
         .
        </li>
        <li>
         Create the port forward entries in your router.
        </li>
        <li>
         Test that your ports are forwarded correctly.
        </li>
       </ol>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       If you are trying to change your NAT Type be sure to read our
       <a href="https://portforward.com/nat-types/">
        NAT Types Defined
       </a>
       guide. This is the most comprehensive definition of gaming console NAT Types on the internet. Most people get NAT Type Open by using
       <a href="https://portforward.com/store/pfconfig.cgi">
        Network Utilities
       </a>
       .
      </p>
      <h3>
       Portforward Guides
      </h3>
      <p>
       We have detailed instructions for each of these steps customized for every router. To get started, visit our
       <a href="https://portforward.com/router.htm">
        List of Routers
       </a>
       and pick your router. Then follow the steps in the guide and you should have an open port.
      </p>
