      <p>
       Welcome to the eLinux wiki!
      </p>
      <p>
       The purpose of this wiki is to preserve and present information about the development and use of Linux in embedded systems as well as open source projects and tools for general embedded development. To use this wiki, click on one of the portal links below.
      </p>
      <p>
       <img alt="Note" height="22" src="/images/thumb/6/60/Bulbgraph.png/18px-Bulbgraph.png" srcset="/images/thumb/6/60/Bulbgraph.png/27px-Bulbgraph.png 1.5x, /images/thumb/6/60/Bulbgraph.png/36px-Bulbgraph.png 2x" title="Note" width="18"/>
       <b>
        Note:
       </b>
       This site has slides, and links to videos, for many years of the Embedded Linux Conference and Japan Jamboree!
      </p>
      <pre>See <a href="/ELC_Presentations" title="ELC Presentations">ELC_Presentations</a> for quick links to pages with information from each event.
