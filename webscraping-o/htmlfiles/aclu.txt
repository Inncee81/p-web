           <h2 class="screenreader-only" data-v-4dedd627="">
            Spotlight
           </h2>
           <div class="column is-full-desktop pb-none-touch is-stretched-touch spotlight" data-v-4dedd627="">
            <img alt="An older person holding a younger person's hand" class="spotlight__image placeholder--lazyload" data-srcset="https://wp.api.aclu.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Reconciliation-2800x1400-1-1200x600.jpg 1200w,https://wp.api.aclu.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Reconciliation-2800x1400-1-2800x1400.jpg 2800w,https://wp.api.aclu.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Reconciliation-2800x1400-1-1000x500.jpg 1000w,https://wp.api.aclu.org/wp-content/uploads/2019/10/Reconciliation-2800x1400-1-400x200.jpg 400w" data-v-4dedd627=""/>
           </div>
           <div class="column is-1-desktop is-hidden-touch" data-v-4dedd627="">
           </div>
           <div class="column is-10-desktop is-full-touch" data-v-4dedd627="">
            <div class="is-row-reversed columns is-multiline" data-v-4dedd627="">
             <div class="column is-5-desktop pl-xl-desktop is-full-touch spotlight__title has-text-right-desktop is-size-1-desktop is-size-3-touch has-font-family-compressed is-uppercase" data-v-4dedd627="">
              Systemic Equality
             </div>
             <div class="column is-7-desktop is-full-touch spotlight__content is-special-size-21 is-size-5-touch" data-v-4dedd627="">
              <div data-v-4dedd627="">
               <p>
                Since the nation’s founding, the fabric of American society has been woven with deeply racist policies, practices, and attitudes that harm Black and Indigenous people of color. These policies have led to an unequal system where white people have both implicit and explicit advantages because of the color of their skin, leading to better opportunities in jobs, education, and housing.
               </p>
               <p>
                <a href="https://www.aclu.org/news/topic/systemic-equality-addressing-americas-legacy-of-racism-and-systemic-discrimination/">
                 Systemic Equality
                </a>
                is a racial justice agenda that seeks to address America’s legacy of racism and systemic discrimination through administrative and legislative campaigns
                <a href="https://www.aclu.org/news/racial-justice/ending-systemic-racism-requires-ensuring-systemic-equality">
                 targeting the Biden-Harris administration and Congress
                </a>
                , advocacy efforts, legal strategies to strike down laws and practices that exclude and harm, and a significant investment and dedicated resources in the South.
               </p>
              </div>
