      <h2 id="can-a-person-with-bad-credit-apply-for-installment-loans">
       Can a Person With Bad Credit Apply for Installment Loans?
      </h2>
      <p>
       An individual with a bad credit record can avail of installment loans. You're concerned about what sort of loan is perfect for your money need. This post discusses the modern cash advances most lenders are offering. Several loans are intended for consumers who are in search for small installment loans.
      </p>
      <p>
       Some are in search of cash advances with poor credit records, while others are searching for financial aid that one can pay on their next salary. If you think applying for a loan is the solution with your present situation, continue reading this post and find out which type of loans will is best for you.
      </p>
      <h2 id="understanding-installment-loans">
       Understanding Installment Loans
      </h2>
      <p>
       An installment loan online is a kind of debt that one can repay over a period of time. It is a bit shorter as compared to others. Also, this sort of loan has fixed interest rates. Since you can easily predict how much you will pay, one can prepare the money needed for repayment ahead of time. It is what makes it different from payday loans.
      </p>
      <p>
       Payday loans are sometimes referred to as salary loans, but the time of repayment is much shorter, which is for several weeks to one month. Some lending firms may oblige you to repay the money borrowed in a lump sum — the reason why it is called payday loans because it is being paid for the next salary date.
      </p>
      <p>
       The main difference between installment and payday loans is the APR or annual percentage rate – it is the amount added to the money you owe. Based on the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, the usual fee for $100 is $15. If paid within two weeks, it will be equivalent to roughly 400% APR. It is not what you expect for a monthly payment — the fact that it is not paid back monthly but only after several days.
      </p>
      <p>
       Installment loans are much different, as one can pay it monthly for an assigned period. The APR varies from one institution to another, as per the company's policy. Generally, if one has an impressive credit record, the APR is much lower. For anyone with bad credit, the APR is not what you expect it to be. The good thing about it is that they are paid for a fixed period that one agreed with your lender.
      </p>
      <p>
       With a credit like this, you can manage your finances and helps one deal with monthly dues. By consistently repaying the due amount on time, it will boost your credit record and obtain a better score.
      </p>
      <h2 id="online-installment-loans-for-bad-credit">
       Online Installment Loans for Bad Credit
      </h2>
      <p>
       One can consider applying for online installment loans for bad credit, even if it is not the most exciting thing to do on your list. However, if you fail to achieve the credit score needed, it is your only choice. If you get approved, it will give you great relief. The requirements and procedure of availing a loan through the internet and traditionally are similar. The only difference is that it is purely done online.
      </p>
      <p>
       Finding the best lending company is not easy at all, particularly for individuals with bad credit. One can either ask a professional to assist them in dealing with their financial problem. Lending companies online, credit unions and local banks offer online installment loans for bad credit.
      </p>
      <p>
       Cash advances for credit-challenged consumers can be secured or unsecured. One of the common characteristics of this type of loan is the high-interest rate.
      </p>
      <h2 id="standard-features-of-installment-loans-online-no-credit-check">
       Standard Features of Installment Loans Online No Credit Check
      </h2>
      <ul type="disc">
       <li>
        Installment loans online no credit check works just like other loans.
       </li>
       <li>
        The money you can lend may start from a few hundred up to thousands of dollars.
       </li>
       <li>
        The payment schedules are done every month for a specified date on a settled duration that ranges from a few months to years.
       </li>
       <li>
        The different interest rates are dependent on a money index so that the price may increase over time.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Interest rates, as well as terms and conditions, will vary from one firm to another. However, installment loans no credit check usually have the following rates:
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Bigger interest rates:
       </strong>
       The company may charge the applicants with low credit scores, a bigger interest as compared to debtors with impressive credit records. For instance, a firm design a loan term based on the applicant's credit history as well as other factors.
      </p>
      <p>
       <strong>
        Higher fees:
       </strong>
       The installment loans online no credit check usually have soaring fees. For instance, a lending company that specializes in bad credit loans could add other fees while the lender offers loans to debtors with good credit without an administrative fee.
      </p>
      <h2 id="is-payday-loans-similar-to-payday-installment-loans">
       Is Payday Loans Similar to Payday Installment Loans?
      </h2>
      <p>
       Payday installment loans are different from payday loans in several ways. They differ on payment terms, fees, interest, and the cash you can loan. The APR for this sort of credit can be around 400%. With payday, the payment is made in a lump sum on the coming paycheck. While with payday installment loans, the payment is made in a divided amount at a fixed rate and on a specified date.
      </p>
      <p>
       Several lending firms of small installment loans may not have a clear distinction between a payday loan and the payday installment loans online. So, if searching for one, don't simply concentrate on the manner the lending company label it, check on the APR, and the way of paying to determine the sort of loan one will get and if it is the best for you.
      </p>
      <h2 id="qualification-for-bad-credit-installment-loans">
       Qualification for Bad Credit Installment Loans
      </h2>
      <p>
       Requirements and qualifications for bad credit installment loans differ from one lender to another. Most of the time, one will be asked to supply the following.
      </p>
      <ul type="disc">
       <li>
        Your complete name
       </li>
       <li>
        Age and identification card
       </li>
       <li>
        Information and proof of your monthly income
       </li>
       <li>
        SSS number
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       Since your credit score is not as impressive as the others, you might get approved but with bigger interest rates. You can look for lending companies that provide less focus on credit checks, as this may affect your credit standing.
