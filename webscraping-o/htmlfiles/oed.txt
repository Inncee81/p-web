              March 2021 update
             </h2>
             <p>
              <span style='display: inline !important; float: none; background-color: rgb(255, 255, 255); color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-family: Arial,"Helvetica Neue",Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12px; font-size-adjust: none; font-stretch: normal; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: normal; letter-spacing: normal; line-height: 1.5em; orphans: 2; text-align: left; text-decoration: none; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px;'>
              </span>
             </p>
             <p style="color: rgb(0, 0, 0); font-family: Arial,&amp;quot;Helvetica Neue&amp;quot;,Helvetica,sans-serif; font-size: 12px; font-style: normal; font-variant: normal; font-weight: 400; letter-spacing: normal; margin-bottom: 1.5em; margin-left: 0px; margin-right: 0px; margin-top: 0px; orphans: 2; text-align: left; text-decoration: none; text-indent: 0px; text-transform: none; -webkit-text-stroke-width: 0px; white-space: normal; word-spacing: 0px;">
              <strong>
               Our latest update:
              </strong>
              over 1,400 new words, sub-entries, and revisions have been added to the Oxford English Dictionary in
              <a href="https://public.oed.com/updates/" style="color: rgb(91, 133, 177);" target="_blank">
               our latest update
              </a>
              , including
              <em>
               gender pay gap,
              </em>
              <em>
               me-too
              </em>
              ,
              <em>
               essential worker
              </em>

              </em>
              .

              <strong>
               Release notes:
              </strong>
              learn more about the words added to the OED this quarter in our
              <a href="https://public.oed.com/blog/the-oed-march-2021-update/" target="_blank" title="OED new words notes March 2021">
               new word notes
              </a>
              by OED Revision Editor, Jonathan Dent.
              <br/>
              <strong>
               Release notes:
              </strong>
              read about
              <a href="https://public.oed.com/blog/the-long-tale-of-a-shirttail/" target="_blank" title="Revision of shirttail blog post">
               the revision of
               <em>
                shirttail
               </em>
              </a>
              in our blog post by OED Executive Editor, Grame Diamond.
              <br/>
              <strong>
               Release notes:

              discover
              <a href="https://public.oed.com/blog/the-x-factor-the-challenges-of-pronouncing-x/" target="_blank" title="Pronunciation of womxn blog post">
               how our pronunciation editors are documenting words containing an X
              </a>
              in an article by Head of Pronunciations, Catherine Sangster.
              <br/>
              <strong>
               Release notes:
              </strong>
              find out about the
              <a href="https://public.oed.com/blog/introducing-bermudian-english/" target="_blank" title="Bermudian English words in the OED">
               Bermudian English words that have been added to the OED
              </a>
              this quarter in a fascinating article by Bermudian English consultant, Dr Rosemary Hall.
             </p>
