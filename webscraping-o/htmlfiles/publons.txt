          <h1 id="over-2-000-000-researchers">
           Over 2,000,000 researchers
          </h1>
         </div>
        </div>
        <div class="row sqs-row">
         <div class="col sqs-col-6 span-6">
          <div class="sqs-block html-block sqs-block-html" data-block-type="2" id="block-5f60705a182fb29ce925">
           <div class="sqs-block-content">
            <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">
             Use Publons to track your publications, citation metrics, peer reviews, and journal editing work in a single, easy-to-maintain profile.
            </p>
            <ul data-rte-list="default">
             <li>
              <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">
               <strong>
                All your publications
               </strong>
               , instantly imported from
               <em>
                Web of Science
               </em>
               , ORCID, or your bibliographic reference manager (e.g. EndNote or Mendeley).
              </p>
             </li>
             <li>
              <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">
               <strong>
                Trusted citation metrics,
               </strong>
               automatically imported from the
               <em>
                Web of Science Core Collection.
               </em>
              </p>
             </li>
             <li>
              <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">
               <strong>
                Correct author attribution,
               </strong>
               <em>
               </em>
               with your unique
               <a href="http://researcherid.com/" target="_blank">
                <em>
                 ResearcherID
                </em>
               </a>
               automatically added to the publications you claim in
               <em>
                Web of Science
               </em>
               <strong>
               </strong>
               collections.
              </p>
             </li>
             <li>
              <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">
               <strong>
                Your verified peer review and journal editing history
               </strong>
               , powered by partnerships with thousands of scholarly journals.
              </p>
             </li>
             <li>
              <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">
               <strong>
                Publons CV
               </strong>
               summarising your scholarly impact as an author, editor and peer reviewer.
              </p>
             </li>
            </ul>
            <p class="" style="white-space:pre-wrap;">
             Want to know more?
             <a href="/benefits/researchers">
              Watch the video
             </a>
             .
            </p>
           </div>
