   <h2 class="GQ5s5" id="how-do-i-stay-safe-when-dating-online">
    How do I Stay Safe When Dating Online?
   </h2>
   <p>
    With online dating continuing to grow, so also the dangers with it. It’s important to keep in mind that while searching for love, staying safe should come first. This review provides some helpful tips on how to stay safe while dating online.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="be-careful-with-your-personal-information">
    Be Careful With Your Personal Information
   </h3>
   <p>
    Feeling the butterflies of finally meeting someone you connect with is thrilling, but it is important to know the amount of personal information you share. It is not advisable to give out private info about yourself to people you just met online. Get to know them first, and even then, don’t overshare.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="organize-your-meeting-in-a-public-place">
    Organize Your Meeting in a Public Place
   </h3>
   <p>
    When meeting someone you recently encountered online for the first time, it is preferable not to meet in a secluded place. Instead, arrange to see one another in a place with lots of people around. For instance, you can meet in a restaurant, cafe or bar.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="inform-your-friend">
    Inform Your Friend
   </h3>
   <p>
    Ensure you inform family or friends before leaving to meet a date. Let them know where you are going, when to expect you, who you are going with, and any other relevant detail.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="you-shouldnt-be-afraid-to-leave">
    You shouldn’t Be Afraid to Leave
   </h3>
   <p>
    Is a date making you uncomfortable? Does he or she make rude and offensive comments? Don’t hesitate to leave. There are millions of people out there you can forge a relationship with so, don’t get stuck with an unpleasant person.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="travel-there-and-back-by-yourself">
    Travel There and Back by Yourself
   </h3>
   <p>
    Do not accept an invitation to carpool; drive your car, or take public transportation if you don’t own a car. By transporting yourself, you have control over when to leave if a dangerous or uncomfortable situation pops up.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="how-do-i-have-a-successful-first-date">
    How do I Have a Successful First Date?
   </h3>
   <p>
    Dating is neither simple nor straightforward, especially in this present age. With the right strategy, you can avoid some common mistakes that lots of people make when going on their first date:
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="avoid-social-media-stalking">
    Avoid Social Media Stalking
   </h3>
   <p>
    Stir clear of the unhealthy act of stalking your date on social media. It is extremely creepy and gives out negative vibes, especially to your date. Of course, you can look up some information about your potential date, so you’re not completely clueless, but don’t let it degenerate into chasing or stalking.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="you-should-choose-comfort">
    You Should Choose Comfort
   </h3>
   <p>
    When going on a first, there’s no need to go to a five-star restaurant or some overpriced bar. Opt for simplicity; have a cup of coffee at a location that’s both convenient and comfortable for both you and your date. By choosing a comfy venue, you’re able to avoid the discomfort often associated with overly expensive date locations and stay within your budget.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="try-the-exercise-with-confidence">
    Try the Exercise with Confidence
   </h3>
   <p>
    Are you shy? Insecure? Or you suck at making conversations? Try some exercise that’ll boost your self-esteem. Listen to relaxing music, learn to make eye contacts, try breathing exercises or yoga, and maintain general positivity.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="have-a-laugh">
    Have a Laugh
   </h3>
   <p>
    Nobody wants to have a conversation with a grumpy and stoic date. That being said, try to smile more and have a good laugh. Smiling is one of the habits of likable people, a sign of happiness, and it helps to project friendly aura. If you’re not the smiley type, practice before going on your first date, but be careful not to come across as phony to your date.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="use-a-body-language">
    Use a Body Language
   </h3>
   <p>
    Understand the way you present yourself during the first date because first impressions last long. Be aware of your body language and be yourself as much as you can; pretending won’t help the relationship in the long run.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="pay-attention">
    Pay Attention
   </h3>
   <p>
    While on a first date, don’t be oblivious; lend attention to your date’s subtle signs, body language, and little mannerisms. Don’t be the only one doing all the talking, listen more to what your date is saying; don’t just nod your head, give intelligent replies. By listening more, you get to know more about your potential partner.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="be-optimistic">
    Be Optimistic
   </h3>
   <p>
    Nobody appreciates pessimists or complainers. Smile, laugh and surround yourself with good thoughts. Maintain an appreciative mindset before going on your date.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="split-the-bills">
    Split the Bills
   </h3>
   <p>
    Ensure you go with your wallet. Don’t wait on your date, offer to pay too, it shows you’re financially independent and responsible. Don’t order the most expensive dish on the menu. It portrays you in a negative light; you can order the same thing as your date.
   </p>
   <h3 class="ye0PYOJdtnc" id="follow-up">
    Follow Up
   </h3>
   <p>
    After a nice date, you can send a decent text to your date. You can set up another meeting, but don’t be impatient, or you might seem desperate.
   </p>
   <h2 class="GQ5s5" id="spam-bots">
    Spam &amp; Bots
   </h2>
   <p>
    Spambots are computer-generated programs that help in spreading spam over the internet. Spambots continually collect people’s contact information, create fake accounts, and run stolen social media accounts. While using free online dating sites, it is normal to come across spams bots, fake accounts, and even more serious things like a scam. You might also encounter unsolicited: product advertisements, weblinks, and messages. However, by choosing a safe and secure dating site, you can avoid spambots and everything associated with it.
   </p>

    What do Experts Think about Dating Sites?

Online dating is now the second most popular way to find and connect with a potential partner. Although online dating has its downsides, it doesn’t take away the fact that it is extremely convenient and easy.
