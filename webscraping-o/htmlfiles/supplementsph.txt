There are two main reasons for supplements overdose: either the number of substances differs on the label and in reality, or you get enough healthy vitamins and minerals from food, so usage of supplements is not needed. That's why organism can be as well overdosed with important substances as suffer from a lack of it.
        </p>

         Here are some examples of side effects:
        </p>

         </li>
         <li>
          The extensive use of vitamin B can cause diarrhea and nausea;
         </li>
         <li>
          Reaching toxic level with vitamin D can be detrimental to bone health and raise blood levels;
         </li>
         <li>
          Too much fish oil in your body may lead to gastrointestinal upset and an increase in bad cholesterol.
         </li>
        </ul>

          Always consult with your doctor or health care adviser before starting to add supplements in your diet;
         </li>
