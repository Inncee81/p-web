          <a href="https://vimeo.com/ondemand/holymodalrounderswfmu" title="&lt;b&gt;The Holy Modal Rounders Documentary “Bound to Lose” is Now Available On Demand&lt;/b&gt;">
           <b>
            The Holy Modal Rounders Documentary “Bound to Lose” is Now Available On Demand
           </b>
          </a>
         </h3>
         <p>
          <strong>
           40 years of unpredictable live shows, drug addictions &amp; breakdowns!
          </strong>
          Psychedelic folk rock legends, The Holy Modal Rounders survived one of the strangest careers in music. Follow the story of duo Peter Stampfel and Steve Weber, who began performing together on the Lower East Side of New York City in the early 1960s. Their unique blend of folk music revival and psychedelia gave them a cult-like following from the late 1960s into the 1970s.
          <a href="https://vimeo.com/ondemand/holymodalrounderswfmu" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">
           Follow their trip in the documentary “Bound to Lose” now available on demand
          </a>
          . 50% of the proceeds to benefit the station you love to love.
         </p>
