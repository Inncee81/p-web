          <h2>
           Meet Pei-Ling
          </h2>
          <p>
           Pei-Ling Hsu, Ph.D., draws on her time as a high school earth science teacher to create “project-based learning” metho​ds for her students at The University of Texas at El Paso, which they use to create engaging science projects for area K-12 students. She has taught different education courses at the undergraduate, master’s and doctoral levels.
          </p>
