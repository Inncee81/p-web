Mysteriously, humans do not need to do either of these when learning to ride a bicycle.       <p>
       Does sunspot activity cause disagreements in the soyabean futures market? According to a Feb. 2021 paper in the journal
       <em>
        Economic Modelling
       </em>

      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        [â¦] theoretically, the information of sunspots causes disagreement in the soybeans futures market mainly by three channels: gradual information flow, limited attention, and heterogeneous priors.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       The new study, from
       <a href="https://www.uni-goettingen.de/en/mr.+hanjie+wang/595204.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
        Mr. Hanjie Wang
       </a>
       ,
       <a href="https://www.uni-goettingen.de/de/jun.-prof.+dr.+jan-henning+feil/187880.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
        Jun.-Prof. Dr. Jan-Henning Feil
       </a>
       and
       <a href="https://www.uni-goettingen.de/de/prof.+xiaohua+yu+ph.d./114620.html" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
        Prof. Xiaohua Yu Ph.D.
       </a>
       at the Georg-August-UniversitÃ¤t GÃ¶ttingen, Germany, examined the implications of
       <a href="https://scholar.harvard.edu/files/stein/files/jep-2007.pdf" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
        âDisagreement Theoryâ
       </a>
       (Hong, Stein, 2007) with regard to soya pricing.
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        [â¦] to analyze investorsâ behaviors given the complicated information of sunspots. Theoretically, disagreements could change market price trends and volatilities. Disagreement on the information of sunspots would shape investorsâ behavior.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       The empirical results of the study highlight the effect of sunspots information on the volatility of soybeans future price.
      </p>
      <blockquote>
       <p>
        Based on the disagreement theory, the underlying mechanism of this phenomenon is that the disagreements exist on the information of sunspot activity among investors of the soybeans futures market. When the sunspots are in a normal status, the weather would be mild for soybean production, and the disagreement on the effects would be small. When the sunspots activities are in an extreme status, either extremely low or extremely high, the disagreement would be high as well.
       </p>
      </blockquote>
      <p>
       See :
       <a href="https://www.sciencedirect.com/science/article/abs/pii/S0264999319310119" rel="noopener" target="_blank">
