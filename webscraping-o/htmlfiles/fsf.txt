             <h4 id="fsf-tag2">
              Free software means that the users have the freedom to
              <a href="//www.gnu.org/philosophy/free-sw.html" style="color:black;">
               <strong>

               </strong>

           <h2>
            Our initiatives
           </h2>
           <ul class="initiatives-list">
            <li class="initiative-item yui3-u-1-7">
             <a href="//defectivebydesign.org?pk_campaign=fsfredesign">
              <span class="img-wrapper">
               <img alt="Defective By Design" src="//static.fsf.org/nosvn/dbd/sites/www.defectivebydesign.org/files/sites/nodrm.civicactions.net/files/images/dbd_color_logo_trim_square.png"/>
              </span>
              <div class="initiatives-description">
               <p>
                Defective by Design is a grassroots campaign to eliminate Digital Restrictions Management (DRM) in media and devices.
               </p>
              </div>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="initiative-item yui3-u-1-7">
             <a href="//emailselfdefense.fsf.org?pk_campaign=fsfredesign">
              <span class="img-wrapper">
               <img alt="Email Self-Defense" src="//static.fsf.org/nosvn/enc-dev0/img/en/screenshots/step2a-01-make-keypair.png"/>
              </span>
              <div class="initiatives-description">
               <p>
                Read the Email Self-Defense Guide to get started with email encryption, a skill necessary to combat bulk surveillance.
               </p>
              </div>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="initiative-item yui3-u-1-7">
             <a href="//directory.fsf.org?pk_campaign=fsfredesign">
              <span class="img-wrapper">
               <img alt="Free Software Directory" src="//static.fsf.org/nosvn/logos/fsd_logo_stacked.svg"/>
              </span>
              <div class="initiatives-description">
               <p>
                The Free Software Directory is a collaborative catalog of computer programs and apps that are fully free as in freedom.
               </p>
              </div>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="initiative-item yui3-u-1-7">
             <a href="//gnu.org?pk_campaign=fsfredesign">
              <span class="img-wrapper">
               <img alt="GNU Project" src="//static.fsf.org/nosvn/logos/campaigns_gnu.small.png"/>
              </span>
              <div class="initiatives-description">
               <p>
                The GNU operating system is a continuously evolving, complete operating system made entirely of free software.
               </p>
              </div>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="initiative-item yui3-u-1-7">
             <a href="//libreplanet.org?pk_campaign=fsfredesign">
              <span class="img-wrapper">
               <img alt="LibrePlanet" src="//static.fsf.org/nosvn/logos/lp-logo-square.svg"/>
              </span>
              <div class="initiatives-description">
               <p>

                <a href="//libreplanet.org/conference">
                 annual conference
                </a>
                , and online collaboration spaces.
               </p>
              </div>
             </a>
            </li>
            <li class="initiative-item yui3-u-1-7">
             <a href="//www.fsf.org/licensing?pk_campaign=fsfredesign">

             </a>

            <li class="initiative-item yui3-u-1-7">
