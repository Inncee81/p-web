      <p>
       <b>
        The Standard Performance Evaluation Corporation (SPEC)
       </b>
       is a
      non-profit corporation formed to establish, maintain and endorse
      standardized benchmarks and tools to evaluate performance and energy 
      efficiency for the newest generation of computing systems. SPEC develops benchmark
      suites and also reviews and publishes submitted results from our
       <a href="/consortium/">
        member
      organizations
       </a>
       and other benchmark licensees.
      </p>
