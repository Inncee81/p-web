        Political Union
       </h1>
      </header>
      <div class="entry-content mh-clearfix">
       <div>
        <p>
         A
         <b>
          political union
         </b>
         is a type of state which is composed of or created out of smaller states. The process is called
         <b>
          unification
         </b>
         . Unifications of states that used to be together and are reuniting is referred to as
         <b>
          reunification
         </b>
         .
        </p>
        <p>
         Unlike a personal union or real union, the individual states share a central government and the union is recognized internationally as a single political entity. A political union may also be called a legislative union or state union.
        </p>
