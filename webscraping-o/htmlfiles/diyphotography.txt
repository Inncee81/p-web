        <p>
         The whole manual vs auto thing for me isn’t really much of a debate. And whether you prefer to use auto or manual, you’re still going to need to understand the underlying principles of photography and how your camera “sees” a scene in order to get the most out of it and the best possible images you can that achieve your vision.
        </p>

         Coming to you from
         <a data-wpel-link="external" href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UC9lyUdwosxq_OxC1uggjUPA" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
          Paul at Photo Genius
         </a>
         , this video compares the differences when shooting multiple scenes using both manual and auto exposure controls. It’s a good illustration of why automatic isn’t perfect for everything (or potentially even most stuff, depending on what you like to shoot) and how understanding manual can get you that control you need.

        <p>
         In a world filled with conspiracy theories these days, there’s one old one that still just refuses to die. The number of people who still believe the moon landings didn’t happen is still pretty high – which should come as no surprise given the path the planet’s taken over the last year or six.
        </p>
        <p>
         But this video from
         <a data-wpel-link="external" href="https://www.youtube.com/channel/UCPDXXXJj9nax0fr0Wfc048g" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
          College Humor
         </a>
         popped up on
         <a data-wpel-link="external" href="https://www.facebook.com/watch/?v=583936079246742" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
          their Facebook page
         </a>
         yesterday explaining why faking them simply could not have been possible. It was originally posted to their YouTube channel
         <a data-wpel-link="external" href="https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=zhp-FTYSGe8" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
          a while ago
         </a>
         , and they even got Emmy Award-winning forensic motion picture analyst
         <a data-wpel-link="external" href="https://www.imdb.com/name/nm1063430/" rel="nofollow noopener" target="_blank">
          Mark Shubin
         </a>
         to explain why the footage definitely wasn’t shot in a studio using real demonstrable physics of light that you can try yourself if you don’t believe him.
        </p>
        <p>
         <a class="more-link" data-wpel-link="internal" href="https://www.diyphotography.net/the-moon-landing-couldnt-have-been-faked-because-the-required-lighting-gear-simply-didnt-exist/">
