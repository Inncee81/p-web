       <p>
        <b>
         <a class="external text" href="http://www.openstreetmap.org/">
          OpenStreetMap
         </a>
        </b>
        is an initiative to create and provide free geographic data, such as street maps, to anyone. The OpenStreetMap Foundation is an international not-for-profit organization supporting, but not controlling, the OpenStreetMap Project.  It is dedicated to encouraging the growth, development and distribution of free geospatial data and to providing geospatial data for anyone to use and share.
        <i>
         <a class="external text" href="http://blog.osmfoundation.org/about/" rel="nofollow">
          Read more about the foundation
         </a>
        </i>
