     <div class="subheading">
      Mission
     </div>
     <br class="mobile-br"/>
     <p class="statements-body">
      H-Net: Humanities and Social Sciences Online (H-Net) is an independent,
                    non-profit scholarly
                    association that offers an open academic space for scholars, teachers, advanced students and related
                    professionals. Built around an online system of networks
                    moderated by certified editors, H-Net is uniquely situated to encourage technological innovation in
                    the humanities and social sciences while safeguarding academic best practices.
     </p>
    </div>
    <div id="values-container">
     <img alt="Hands Raising a Heart" class="statements-images3" src="img/values_icon.png"/>
     <div class="values-text">
      <div class="subheading">
       Values
      </div>
      <br class="mobile-br"/>
      <div class="values-body">
       <p>
        H-Net's work is guided and informed by our commitment to:
        <br/>
        <br/>
        - high standards of scholarly, pedagogical and professional behavior
        <br/>
        <br/>
        - a pluralistic and democratic way of decision-making
        <br/>
        <br/>
        - principles of Open Access
       </p>
