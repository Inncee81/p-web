The Raise the Wage Act of 2021 would help eliminate poverty-level wages by raising the national minimum wage to $15 per hour by 2025. This raise is long overdue and would deliver broad benefits to workers and the economy.

							});
							$container[0].normalize();
						}

					});
				}
