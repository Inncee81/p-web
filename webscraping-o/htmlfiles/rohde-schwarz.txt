      </h1>

      </h2>
      <p>
       Rohde &amp; Schwarz develops, produces and markets a wide range of electronic capital goods for industry, infrastructure operators and government customers. The independent group is among the technology and market leaders in all of its business fields, including wireless communications and RF test and measurement, broadcast and media, air traffic control and military radiocommunications, cybersecurity and network technology.
      </p>
      <p>
       In fiscal year 2019/2020, Rohde &amp; Schwarz generated EUR 2.58 billion in revenue. The company owes this tremendous success to its 12,300 highly qualified employees in over 70 countries.
