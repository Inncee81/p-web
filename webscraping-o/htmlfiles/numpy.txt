    <div class="keyfeatures-box-container">
     <div class="keyfeatures-box-content keyfeatures-underline">
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-title">
       Powerful N-dimensional arrays
      </div>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-text">
       Fast and versatile, the NumPy vectorization, indexing, and broadcasting concepts are the de-facto standards of array computing today.
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="keyfeatures-box-content keyfeatures-underline">
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-title">
       Numerical computing tools
      </div>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-text">
       NumPy offers comprehensive mathematical functions, random number generators, linear algebra routines, Fourier transforms, and more.
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="keyfeatures-box-content keyfeatures-underline">
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-title">
       Interoperable
      </div>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-text">
       NumPy supports a wide range of hardware and computing platforms, and plays well with distributed, GPU, and sparse array libraries.
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="keyfeatures-box-content keyfeatures-underline">
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-title">
       Performant
      </div>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-text">
       The core of NumPy is well-optimized C code. Enjoy the flexibility of Python with the speed of compiled code.
      </div>
     </div>
     <div class="keyfeatures-box-content keyfeatures-underline">
      <p>
      </p>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-title">
       Easy to use
      </div>
      <div class="keyfeatures-box-text">
       NumPyâ€™s high level syntax makes it accessible and productive for programmers from any background or experience level.
      </div>
     </div>
