       <h2 class="title" style="text-align: center">
        How to find a Pick-Your-Own farm
       </h2>
       <p>
        Whether you call it pick-your-own, PYO or U-pick, it's easy to find one 
	  near you!  There is a "
        <a href="#states" target="_top">
         Start 
	  here
        </a>
