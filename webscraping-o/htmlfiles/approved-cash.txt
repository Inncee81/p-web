        <h2 id="">
        </h2>
        <p>
         Financial difficulties, bank overdraft, urgent need for cash, unexpected costs, payment of taxes … Sometimes, for unexpected reasons, it is difficult to wait until the end of the month to receive your salary. By definition, wages are paid in return for work performed. However, to respond to this type of problem, such companies as Approved Cashed introduced a service of the payment of advance wages. There are two types of it, which must be distinguished:  payday loans and cash advance. What to choose?
        </p>
        <p>
         While talking about short-term loans, payday loans and cash advance are two types of options that are used most often by customers. They provide applicants not only with a quick access to the money lent, but they also come with rather moderate lending requirements.
        </p>
        <p>
         Therefore, even if you are an owner of a bad credit history and cannot apply for other types of online loans, these two variants might be open for you. Considering the fact that people with poor credit usually need money more frequently, these two types of cash loans online are a real godsend.
        </p>
        <p>
         But which type is better for you in the case “I need a loan” urgently? Let’s take a closer look.
        </p>
        <p>
         A payday loan is a credit for your forthcoming salary. For instance, you need to repair something in your house, but cannot afford buying the necessary materials, because your salary will be only in 2 weeks. Here, payday loans online come to help you.
        </p>
        <p>
         Such a service was specially introduced to give you money for a short period of couple of days and no longer than a few weeks. If you cannot fulfill the obligations, be ready to pay rather high interest charges.
