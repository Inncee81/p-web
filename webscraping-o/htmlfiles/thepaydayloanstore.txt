      <h2 class="js-animate anim-left">
       Payday Loans: What They Are and How They Work
      </h2>
      <p>
       Payday loans are short-term credits that help to solve financial problems quickly. To use the service, you do not need to collect the numerous documents that the bank requires from its customers. The main thing you should remember that it is necessary to repay borrowing after your next payday.
      </p>
      <p>
       The benefits of payday loans no credit check:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        High speed of service. Quick application approval while the money is transferred as soon as one business day.
       </li>
       <li>
        Simple procedure. There is no need to look for a creditor organization. Everything can be done online.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       The disadvantages of the service:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        The interest rate is a key disadvantage of payday loansas it can reach up to 600% APR. However, it is possible to choose acceptable conditions that could be arranged by the period of lending, the amount of money, and the terms of its return.
       </li>
       <li>
        Short-term repayment. It is necessary to repay the debt within 2-4 weeks to avoid any additional fees.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <p>
       If the application is approved, the funds will be transferred to your bank account within 24 hours (if you are claiming for payday loans online.) In case you are approaching a storefront lender, you will receive your cash in about 15 minutes.
      </p>
      <h4 id="how-to-get-approved-for-a-payday-loan-successfully">
       How to Get Approved for a Payday Loan Successfully?
      </h4>
      <p>
       It is fairly simple to get approved for payday loans. All you need is to follow specific requirements. Firstly, you should specify the desired sum you want to borrow. If you need about $100-$1000, the service of the lending companies is just what you need. In case you need a larger sum, you'd better look for another option.
      </p>
      <p>
       If you pick a reliable company, first off, it is necessary to fill in an application form. Whether you approach an in-store lender or online lending service, you will need to provide such info to get payday loans:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        Name/surname
       </li>
       <li>
        Telephone number
       </li>
       <li>
        Revenue details
       </li>
       <li>
        Bank account details
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4 id="how-long-does-it-take-to-get-a-payday-loan-application-approved">
       How Long Does It Take to Get a Payday Loan Application Approved?
      </h4>
      <p>
       Payday loans have their “easy to get” status for several reasons. One of them is fast application approval. In-store lenders will sometimes have a faster approval time compared to online services. Thus, if you address a storefront lending company, you may get your money in 15 minutes. Once you've been approved, you will typically receive your cash right away.
      </p>
      <p>
       For those who want to take payday loans online, things are different. It is necessary to wait several hours for the result. If you've got a positive answer, lenders can deposit the funds into your account within 24 hours.
      </p>
      <h4 id="the-maximum-number-of-payday-loans-a-consumer-can-have">
       The Maximum Number of Payday Loans a Consumer Can Have
      </h4>
      <p>
       The applicant can have no more than two payday loans at a time. Moreover, there are some nuances you should know. It is prohibited to address the same creditor.The lender has to verify a customer's eligibility. But even if the borrower has a stable income, each cash advance will be limited to $600.
      </p>
      <p>
       In case of taking multiple payday loans, the lender will require proof that you have:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        a job and a checking account;
       </li>
       <li>
        unspoiled credit history;
       </li>
       <li>
        a permanent address.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h4 id="extra-charges-for-payday-loans-and-possible-nuances">
       Extra Charges for Payday Loans and Possible Nuances
      </h4>
      <p>
       In case you have failed to repay a cash advance on time, the lender will charge the extra fees, increasing your out-of-pocket costs. It is worth mentioning that the longer you delay the payment, the more you will eventually need to pay.
      </p>
      <p>
       The borrower can have extra charges such as:
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        <b>
         NSF fees
        </b>
        are charged when a customer makes a payment with insufficient funds to cover a payment.
       </li>
       <li>
        A
        <b>
         returned payment fee
        </b>
        is charged if the borrower bounced his/her payment on payday loans. Typically, lending services claim the highest returned payment charges.
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         Rollover interest fee
        </b>
        is charged to delay paying back the cash advance.
       </li>
      </ul>
      <h2 class="js-animate anim-left">
       Payday Loans: Terms and Conditions
      </h2>
      <p>
       The potential borrowers should know the terms and conditions of the majority of lending services before approaching them.
      </p>
      <ul>
       <li>
        The individual should be over 18.
       </li>
       <li>
        The minimum credit score to get payday loans should be no less than 300.

      </h3>
      <p>
       Sometimes lending companies can be uncompromising and reject payday loans. The reasons are different. Read on to find out the most common ones.

         The applicant has multiple unpaid payday loans.

        If you have another existing cash advance, the lender will easily determine it by checking it in the database.         <b>
         The applicant has bounced checks.
        </b>
        When you approach a lending company, the lender can check the last three months of bank statements. If the client has failed to make timely payments or his/her bank account is overdrawn, it may also result in a red flag for approving a payday loans application.
       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         The applicant has a cash paid job.
        </b>

       <li>
        <b>
         The applicant deposits money to gambling websites.
        </b>

       </li>
       <li>
        <b>
         The applicant doesn’t meet the minimum requirements of the credit provider.
        </b>
        Different financial institutions have different criteria. If you, for some reason, don’t meet any requirements of the lending company, for example, you are underage, or you are not a citizen of a particular state, most likely, your application will be denied.
